SnakeTerm is a terminal snake game that uses NCurses library.

In SnakeTerm snake can have length from 3 to 15. Snake can eat one of three types of food:

Normal Food (.)
    Increases snake length by 1
Live Food (+)
    Increases snake lives count by 1
NoWall Food (*)
    Snake can move through walls

If snake lenght is 15 you go to the next level where snake moves faster. After collision with the wall or X-cell snake loses 1 live and level restarts. Game over if lives count have been 0.

Control:

[Left key]  Move left
[Right key] Move right
[Up key]    Move up
[Down key]  Move down
[P]     Pause
[Q]     Quit
